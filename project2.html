FitFix: A prototype closet app

In this project, I contributed to the design of FitFix, a sustainable clothing app for closet tracking, shopping, and outfit inspiration. I designed the shopping experience, including browsing, checkout, and payment pages in Figma.

Skills learned:

  • Creation of a prototype in Figma (screens, components, and basic layouts)
  • Designing a user friendly interface, spacing, readability, and color schemes
  • Creation of a checkout page for an app, including card information and shipping
